About BGD e-GOV CIRT

At BGD e-GOV CIRT (Bangladesh e-Government Computer Incident Response Team), we are at the forefront of securing Bangladesh's digital future. Established in March 2016, following the infamous cyberattack on Bangladesh Bank, our organization was formed as a critical initiative to protect the nation's rapidly expanding digital landscape.

Operating under the Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC) and governed by the Cybersecurity Act of 2023, we act as the National Computer Emergency Response Team (N-CERT) for the country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure the safety of Critical Information Infrastructures (CIIs) and e-government services by mitigating cyber threats and safeguarding digital assets. As Bangladesh continues to embrace digital transformation, BGD e-GOV CIRT has been instrumental in fostering trust in online services and ensuring the security of government networks.

Collaboration & Recognition

With a strong emphasis on collaboration, innovation, and capacity building, we maintain active partnerships with international cybersecurity organizations, contributing to global efforts to combat cyber threats. As a testament to our commitment to excellence:

  • Bangladesh now ranks 36th in the National Cybersecurity Index.
  • Recognized as a Tier-1 Role Model Country in the Global Cybersecurity Index 2024.

We are more than just a response team; we are pioneers in building a secure and resilient digital Bangladesh.
