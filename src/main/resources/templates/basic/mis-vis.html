Our Mission & Vision

Securing Bangladesh’s Digital Future. A Globally Trusted and Cyber-Resilient Bangladesh

Mission

Our mission is to build a secure, resilient, and trusted cyberspace for Bangladesh by proactively detecting and mitigating cyber threats, protecting critical infrastructures, and fostering a culture of cybersecurity awareness, capacity and collaboration.

We are committed to:

  • Protecting national Critical Information Infrastructures (CIIs) and ensuring the security and continuity of e-government services.
  • Building a robust cybersecurity ecosystem through innovation, capacity development, and the strategic use of technology.
  • Strengthening partnerships with national and international stakeholders to confront and overcome evolving cyber challenges together.

Vision

We envision Bangladesh as a global leader in cybersecurity —a nation empowered by innovation, protected by strong digital defenses, and trusted by its people, institutions, and global partners.

Our vision is rooted in:

  • Global Excellence: Setting new standards in cybersecurity operations, governance, and collaboration.
  • Digital Trust: Creating a safe digital environment where citizens and organizations can engage online with confidence.
  • Sustainability: Advancing long-term, adaptive cybersecurity strategies to address tomorrow’s threats.
  • Innovation: Encouraging the adoption of cutting-edge technologies and approaches to stay ahead in the global cyber domain.
