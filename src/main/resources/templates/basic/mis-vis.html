Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles and aspirations for a brighter future.

Our Mission

To create a secure and resilient cyberspace for Bangladesh by proactively identifying and mitigating cyber threats, safeguarding critical infrastructures, and fostering a culture of cybersecurity awareness and collaboration.

We are committed to:

  • Protecting national Critical Information Infrastructures (CIIs) and e-government services.
  • Building a robust cybersecurity ecosystem through training, innovation, and technology.
  • Strengthening cooperation with national and international stakeholders to address emerging cyber challenges.

Our Vision

To position Bangladesh as a global leader in cybersecurity by developing innovative solutions, enhancing cybersecurity capacities, and building a trusted digital environment for all.

Our vision embodies:

  • Global Excellence: Setting benchmarks in cybersecurity practices.
  • Digital Trust: Ensuring citizens and organizations can rely on digital services without fear of cyber threats.
  • Sustainability: Promoting long-term solutions for cybersecurity challenges.
  • Innovation: Encouraging the adoption of cutting-edge technologies to combat cyber risks.
