What We Do

We are more than a response team — we are innovators, defenders, and strategic enablers of Bangladesh’s digital future. Every day, we work to build a safe, resilient, and trusted cyber environment for all. At BGD e-GOV CIRT, every initiative we take is a stride toward a more secure Bangladesh.

Our Activities

At BGD e-GOV CIRT, our activities go beyond response—it’s about building a secure, resilient, and future-ready digital Bangladesh. As the country’s national cybersecurity agency, we deliver comprehensive, strategic, and technical support to safeguard critical infrastructures, digital platforms, and government systems.
From proactive threat monitoring to international partnerships, here’s how we’re shaping Bangladesh’s cybersecurity landscape:

Cyber Threat Intelligence

Anticipating threats before they strike

We serve as the nation’s digital sentinels—constantly scanning the cyber horizon for potential threats. By gathering, analyzing, and disseminating real-time threat intelligence, we empower government institutions to stay ahead of cyber adversaries.

Incident Response and Mitigation

Always ready, always responsive.

Our dedicated incident response team operates 24/7 to detect, analyze, and contain cyber incidents. Whether it’s a data breach, malware attack, or targeted intrusion, we respond swiftly to minimize damage and restore normalcy—safeguarding public services and sensitive data.

Risk-Based IT Security Audits

Evaluating weaknesses - Strengthening defenses

We conduct rigorous IT security audits tailored to each organization’s risk profile. These audits uncover critical vulnerabilities, recommend mitigations, and help institutions meet compliance standards.

Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Think like an attacker - Defend like a pro

Through structured VAPT programs, we simulate real-world attack scenarios to uncover system, network, and application-level vulnerabilities.

Digital Forensics

Uncovering the truth through technology

Our advanced Digital Forensics Lab plays a vital role in cyber investigations—analyzing digital artifacts, tracing intrusions, and supporting legal processes.

Cybersecurity Awareness and Capacity Building

Empowering people, not just systems

Technology alone isn’t enough—cybersecurity begins with awareness. We organize hands-on workshops, simulations, and drills to train professionals in both public and private sectors.

National Data Center Support

Keeping the digital backbone strong

The National Data Center is at the heart of Bangladesh’s e-government infrastructure. We ensure its seamless operation—managing cloud environments, databases, email systems, and digital services for uninterrupted, secure delivery to citizens and agencies.

International Collaboration

Cybersecurity without borders

We actively collaborate with global cybersecurity bodies like FIRST, APCERT, OIC-CERT and others to exchange threat intelligence, share best practices, and build global resilience. These partnerships position Bangladesh as a proactive player on the global cybersecurity stage.

9. Advancing Cybersecurity Architecture

We drive innovation through projects like the Bangladesh National Digital Architecture (BNDA), which streamlines e-services, digital applications, and government systems to improve accessibility and efficiency.

10. Supporting Legislative and Regulatory Initiatives

Our expertise contributes to the development of national policies and regulations, such as the Cybersecurity Act of 2023, to strengthen the country’s cybersecurity framework.
