What We Do

Our Impact at BGD e-GOV CIRT

BGD e-GOV CIRT is a comprehensive cybersecurity organization that provides end-to-end solutions to secure Bangladesh’s critical infrastructures and digital assets. We operate across multiple domains to ensure a secure and efficient cyber ecosystem. Here’s how we make an impact:

1. Cyber Threat Intelligence

We actively monitor, collect, and analyze information about potential cyber threats. By issuing timely notifications and alerts, we empower organizations to take proactive measures against cybersecurity risks. So far, we have sent 632 reports to government institutions, ensuring they stay one step ahead of evolving cyber threats.

2. Incident Response and Mitigation

Our team is on standby 24/7 to respond to cybersecurity incidents. From identifying the source of a breach to mitigating its impact, we ensure swift and effective action to restore normal operations.

3. Risk-Based IT Audits

We conduct in-depth IT audits to evaluate the security posture of key government institutions. By identifying vulnerabilities, we help strengthen their defenses. To date, we’ve completed 35 IT audits, setting a benchmark for IT security standards.

4. Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Through our extensive VAPT programs, we identify weaknesses in systems, networks, and applications. Our efforts have resulted in assessments of 2,246 assets across 32 government institutions, ensuring that vulnerabilities are addressed before they can be exploited.

5. Digital Forensics

Our Digital Forensics Lab plays a pivotal role in investigating cyber incidents by analyzing digital evidence. We’ve worked on 486 pieces of evidence and provided 61 comprehensive reports to assist law enforcement and government agencies.

6. Cybersecurity Awareness and Training

We believe that education is the first line of defense against cyber threats. By conducting workshops, seminars, and cybersecurity drills, we have trained over 5,688 individuals from government and private sectors. These programs equip participants with the knowledge and skills needed to protect their organizations.

7. National Data Center Support

We ensure the seamless operation of the National Data Center, which serves as the backbone for e-government services. Our experts manage the email, cloud, and database systems, ensuring uninterrupted service delivery.

8. International Collaboration

Cybersecurity is a global challenge, and we actively partner with leading international organizations such as FIRST, APCERT, US-CERT, and others. These collaborations help us stay ahead in combating sophisticated cyber threats.

9. Advancing Cybersecurity Architecture

We drive innovation through projects like the Bangladesh National Digital Architecture (BNDA), which streamlines e-services, digital applications, and government systems to improve accessibility and efficiency.

10. Supporting Legislative and Regulatory Initiatives

Our expertise contributes to the development of national policies and regulations, such as the Cybersecurity Act of 2023, to strengthen the country’s cybersecurity framework.
